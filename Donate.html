The Coastal Bend India Association (CBIA) is a 501(c) non-profit dedicated to strengthening the identity and unity of the Indian community in South Texas through cultural celebrations, educational excellence, and community service. Your donation helps us expand programs that celebrate Indian festivals like Diwali and Independence Day, offer scholarships to students, and host community outreach initiatives that connect cultures and generations. As a recognized nonprofit, your contribution is tax-deductible—providing you, the donor, with potential financial benefits while making a lasting impact. Donors who wish to be acknowledged will also be honored during our annual Diwali Festival celebration. Whether you're giving to preserve tradition, promote education, or support meaningful change, your generosity fuels CBIA’s mission—and we are always grateful for your support.
