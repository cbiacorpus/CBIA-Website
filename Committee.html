Arvind Modak

Treasurer

Dr. Arvind Modak is a distinguished medical professional with over 22 years of experience in clinical and administrative medicine. He has a proven track record in primary care, hospitalist roles, long-term care, and academic medicine. Dr. Modak began his career as an employed hospitalist and quickly advanced to founding his own medical group, which was later acquired by a national company. Serving as their CMO, Dr. Modak helped in streamlining transition of care and reducing readmissions. In 2017, Dr. Modak founded Innovative Care Partners, integrating outpatient, inpatient, and long-term care services under one umbrella. This venture was acquired by Cano Health in 2021, where he served as Regional Medical Director until October 2023. He then restarted his private practice, Primary Care Partners, with two locations in Corpus Christi, focusing on providing comprehensive primary care and managing hospitalized patients in LTAC and rehabilitation units. Throughout his career, Dr. Modak has demonstrated exceptional leadership and a commitment to improving patient care. His extensive experience includes managing medical directorships, mentoring residents, and overseeing clinical teams.

Fun Fact:

As a family man, Dr. Modak has the full support of a lovely wife and has raised two boys who are pursuing a career in business and the venture capital (VC) industry. Dr. Modak is an avid golfer, having won the club championship at a local country club, and he is also a keen badminton player.

Sulakshana Modak

Treasurer

Sulakshana (Sulu) Modak is a Practice Manager at Primary Care Partners, a physician-owned practice in Corpus Christi. Born in Maharashtra, India, Sulu has been a resident of Corpus Christi for the last 25 years. She has a bachelor's degree in accounting from Mumbai, India. She also has an Associate's degree in Business from Del Mar College and a BBA from TAMUCC. Currently, she is pursuing her MBA in Business and Healthcare from TAMUCC. Over the years, she has been an active part of the community and served as a President of the Coastal Bend India Association, as well as in various other leadership positions over the last 25 years. She has also served on various Boards like the Indian Temple Organization, Nueces County Medical Alliance, CASA of Coastal Bend just to name a few, bringing various community services projects and advocating for issues that impact local physicians. She believes in the power of education and, as a past president of the Rotary Club of South Side Corpus Christi, she initiated a mental health project called "Are You Okay" in collaboration with CCISD and area high schools to educate and raise awareness about mental health.

Fun Fact:

She has been married to Dr. Arvind Modak for 26 years and is the mother of two wonderful young men.
